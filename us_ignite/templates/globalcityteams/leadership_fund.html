GCTC Leadership Fund


On behalf of the GCTC Partners IBM, Intel and GE, US Ignite is pleased to announce the GCTC Leadership Fund. This fund will make awards to defray travel costs to the GCTC Expo, and it will also provide cash awards to exceptional Action Clusters that advance their projectâ€™s objective as a part of the Global City Teams Challenge.


The GCTC Leadership Fund is available to private companies, local governments, non-profit organizations, and educational institutions, regardless of their geographic location.


The Leadership Fund has two funding priorities:

  • Travel reimbursement for GCTC teams traveling to the GCTC Expo
  • Leadership prizes for notable projects deploying technologically replicable, scalable, and measurable IoT projects in the Smart City sphere

Key Dates and Deadlines:

  • The applications deadline is April 15th, 2016.
  • We encourage the interested parties to schedule a time to meet with representatives of the Leadership Fund at the Tech Jam (link to tech jam registration) and/ or set-up a video call before the April 15th deadline.
  • Travel award announcement will be made by April 30th, 2016.
  • Organizations receiving GCTC Leadership Prizes will be announced on May 15th, 2016.
